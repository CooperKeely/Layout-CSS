Ammount Ingredients
2 Lbs. boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into 1 inch pieces
2 tbsp. Extra virgin olive oil
2 tbsp. Paprika
0.5 tsp. Cumin
1 tsp. red pepper flakes
1 tsp. The zest from one lemon
1-2 tbsp. the juice of one lemon
2 tsp. Kosher Salt
0.5 tsp. Cracked Black Pepper
5 Garlic Cloves
1 Large onion cut into 1 inch chunks
1-2 tbsp. Vegetable oil for greasing the grill

Equiptment

  • skewers (6 to 8 of them)
  • teaspoon
  • knife
  • bowl
  • silicone brush
  • baking sheet
  • a grill

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine the yogurt, olive oil, paprika, cumin, red pepper flakes, lemon zest, lemon juice,salt, pepper and garlic.
  2. Start skewering on your chicken pieces alternating with your pieces of onion place the prepared kebabs on your baking sheet
  3. On the baking sheet lather your chicken skewers with your yogurt marinade with the silicone brush
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat lightly grease your grill grates. Place your kebabs on the grill for about 13-15 minutes intermittently turning them.
  5. Once finished remove the kebabs from the grill and serve
